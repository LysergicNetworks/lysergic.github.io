Nazi Zombie Island


Instructions:

  • Extract nazi_zombie_island folder to C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\Call of Duty World at War\mods*


  • Start Waw

  • Select Mods and select nazi_zombie_island or zombie island

  • SOLO Playing: If you wish to play alone then open console (~) and type "/map nazi_zombie_island".
